Project Overview

The Influence DNA Website Redesign project aimed to revamp Influence DNA's existing website by creating a comprehensive design system and designing web and mobile mockups. Influence DNA, a leading influencer marketing platform, needed a modern, user-friendly, and visually appealing online presence to enhance user engagement and brand perception.

Background

It served as a test prerequisite during my job application process, where I was provided with a project brief and all the necessary assets. The objective was to revamp the entire website and showcase my abilities as a designer. I really had fun in designing the UI in the span of 5 hours and felt proud with my work that I had to include it in my portolio.

Roles

  • UI/UX Designer

Tools

  • Figma
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator

The Problem

The tight deadline was a significant challenge in this project. I had very little time to come up with a concept, create a complete design system, and find the perfect colors, which put a lot of pressure on the creative process.

Another obstacle was that some essential design elements were missing initially. To overcome this, I had to use a technique called "inspect element" on their website to locate and extract these missing assets. This process required careful attention to detail to ensure the final design looked cohesive and visually appealing.

PROTOTYPE

Approach

In dealing with these challenges, I had to be resourceful and make the most of the time and tools available. This project highlighted my ability to adapt to tight schedules and find creative solutions to unexpected problems.
