Challenges

One of the biggest challenge for me was the clients user experience. I had to put myself in their shoes to try and improve the over all experience when our clients use our product. I picture a restaurant full of customers where everything is moving so fast. How would my design help the user in this situation?

I had to do more user-centric research and my findings were majority of our clients are restaurant owners in their 40's and above. Another factor was muscle memory, people who have used the application for longer periods tend to develop muscle memory specially during rush hours.

Lastly, the main challenge was time, they wanted to redesign and roll out new features as soon as possible.

Approach

To tackle these challenges, first, I focused my design to have bigger buttons and texts for easy navigation. Next was to keep all most used buttons in the same place as they were to avoid confusion and keep the muscle memory. And lastly, I worked closely with the developers to avoid any major errors and backlogs
