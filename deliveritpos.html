Challenges

Since the project was a success, the Product Owners wanted the app to be done as quickly as possible. Forcing us designers to create product designs hand in hand with the developers so there was no room for errors.

Another factor was creating the design based on the existing online ordering app. The design was limited because of this request. We had to build a new and fresh design without moving far away from the old design and layout.

Lastly, the main challenge was the existing functions of the online web ordering didn't translate well to mobile view

Approach

To tackle these challenges, the Product Design team was working closely with the developers and QA's to ship high quality work whilst avoiding errors. We also conducted ideation methods like brainstorming with the engineers and product owners and doing consistent daily meetings and standups.
