Project Overview

JLDR Engineering Services is a group of Filipino professional architects and engineers engaged in the architectural design and construction of residential homes and commercial buildings in the Philippines based here in Zambales.

Background

JLDR Engineering Services is seeking a comprehensive brand identity redesign to elevate their online and offline presence, effectively showcase their expertise, and attract potential clients in the architectural and construction industry. The primary goal is to create a modern, memorable, and cohesive brand image without moving away from their old brand that reflects their professionalism, innovation, and commitment to excellence.

Roles

  • Brand Identity Designer
  • Logo Designer
  • Social Media Marketing Designer

Tools

  • Adobe Xd
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator

THE PROBLEM

Despite being a group of talented Filipino architects and engineers with an impressive portfolio, JLDR Engineering Services is struggling with limited online exposure. As a result, they are not reaching their target audience effectively, missing out on potential clients and projects. Their current online presence is minimal, and they have a relatively small following on social media platforms. This lack of online visibility is hindering their growth and preventing them from showcasing their expertise to a wider audience.

Approach

Together with my team, we came up with some templates of visually appealing graphics and social media assets that highlight JLDR's notable projects, innovative designs, and industry insights. These graphics can be shared on various social media platforms, creating engaging content to attract and retain followers. Additionally, we suggested to have a consistent posting schedule to keep the audience informed and interested.
