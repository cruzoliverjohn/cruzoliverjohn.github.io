Hi there!

I'm Oliver, a designer and front-end developer based in the Philippines. With over 5 years of experience under my belt, I specialize in creating exceptional websites, applications, and everything in between. My love for design blossomed when I discovered the world of Android customization, where I tinkered with KWGT, widgets, and third-party launchers. This fascination then extended to desktop customization using Rainmeter. It was during this exploration that I developed a keen eye for good design.

My journey began as a graphic designer, where I honed my creative skills and learned how to translate ideas into captivating visuals. Over time, I realized my passion for bridging the gap between design and development, which led me to transition into front-end design. Combining my artistic flair with technical expertise, I now create seamless user experiences and visually striking interfaces.

Currently, I'm actively involved in designing websites and crafting marketing materials that leave a lasting impression. I constantly strive to stay ahead of the curve, keeping up with the latest design trends and technologies. My goal is to create not just aesthetically pleasing designs but also user-centric experiences that captivate and engage audiences.

I'm thrilled to be a part of this dynamic field, and I'm always excited to take on new challenges and bring innovative ideas to life.
