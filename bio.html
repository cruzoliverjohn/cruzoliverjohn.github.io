Hi there!

I'm Oliver, a Senior Graphic & Web Designer from the Philippines. 6+ years in the design industry, I specialize in crafting websites, apps, and all things in between.

Graphic design was my first love, where I honed my skills in transforming ideas into captivating visuals. But then, the magic of the web pulled me in, and I jumped into the world of front-end design. Currently, I'm designing impactful websites and marketing materials that leave a lasting impression. I'm a constant learner, keeping up with the latest trends to create designs that are not only beautiful but also user-centric, captivating, and engaging.
