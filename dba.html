Background

The creative team was tasked to do a redesign of DBA's current brand identity for the year 2022.

Roles

  • Brand Identity Designer
  • Social Media Marketing Designer

Tools

  • Figma
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator

Challenges

I jumped on the redesign trying to refresh the current branding they've been using which consists of triangles and sharp lines but apparently the design I came up with was really close to the design they've had from the previous year so I have to restart from scratch.

Since i've already used up some time and effort on the first design, I had to go do some quick research and come up with a new design in just a week or less which will be then presented to the product owners and CEO.

Approach

After multiple revisions, I decided to try a different approach in the design. The objective was to create a fun but professional concept identity for the branding. Expanding my creative thought seeing something new and different, I used abstract contemporary modern geometric shapes while retaining the current colors.
