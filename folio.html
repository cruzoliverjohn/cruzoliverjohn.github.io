Logo Meaning

As a believer in minimalism and symmetry, my logo elegantly combines my initials, C.O.J., reflecting this philosophy.

My brand identity draws inspiration from three key personality types: experts, sociable individuals, and artistic personalities. For experts, the minimalist design reflects a focus on clarity and getting to the essence of a concept. Sociable individuals will appreciate the openness and approachability conveyed by the clean lines and symmetry. Artistic personalities will find themselves drawn to the creative flexibility inherent in the minimalist style. By combining these elements, the logo effectively communicates my brand's vision and resonates with a diverse range of individuals.

Approach

Leading by example, I'm applying my minimalist design philosophy to my personal brand. This reflects my ongoing journey of refinement and dedication to improvement. While I may not have a formal design background, my love for the craft fuels my desire to continuously learn and contribute to the field.
